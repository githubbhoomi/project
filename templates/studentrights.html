Student Responsibilities and Rights

As a student in college, you have certain responsibilities and rights that are important to be aware of. These include:

Responsibilities

1. Attend classes regularly and punctually.
2. Complete assignments and submit them on time.
3. Participate in class discussions and activities.
4. Respect teachers, staff, and fellow students.
5. Adhere to the college's code of conduct and policies.

Rights

1. Receive a quality education.
2. Access to necessary resources and facilities.
3. Express opinions and ideas freely.
4. Be treated fairly and without discrimination.
5. Privacy of personal information.

About Discipline

Discipline plays a crucial role in creating a conducive learning environment. It helps in maintaining order, fostering respect, and promoting academic growth. As a student, it is essential to:

  • Follow the college's code of conduct.
  • Respect your teachers and fellow students.
  • Attend classes regularly and be punctual.
  • Adhere to assignment and examination guidelines.
  • Engage in ethical academic practices.
